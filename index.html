About Me

Passionate for imagination and problem-solving, I love to design user-friendly interfaces that captivate users. My ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between creativity and functionality, turning ideas into realities through my coding skills.

Manga

From My Heart - A Journey of Self-Discovery

"From My Heart" is a captivating one-shot manga created in 2021, following the journey of sixteen-year-old May as she embarks on a transformative path of self-discovery. Spanning 52 pages, the comic beautifully intertwines expressive art with compelling storytelling, making May's challenges and triumphs relatable to readers of all ages.

This manga stands as a testament to my growth, merging my art and storytelling skills to craft a heartfelt and inspiring work. The unwavering support of my family and friends throughout the creative process played a vital role in completing this ambitious project.

"From My Heart" emphasizes the significance of self-discovery and its lasting benefits, encouraging readers to embrace their own journeys of self-exploration. The manga's profound themes and unique visuals leave a lasting impression, serving as a reminder of the transformative power of creativity and the importance of pursuing one's dreams.

With its portrayal of personal growth and artistic achievement, "From My Heart" stands as a touching expression of passion, emotions, and the joy of creating meaningful art.
